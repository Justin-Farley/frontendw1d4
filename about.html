About Us

Benny's Bikes is a family-owned bicycle shop located in Hilton Head, South Carolina. We specialize in providing high-quality bicycles, accessories, and repair services to cycling enthusiasts of all levels.

Our mission is to promote a healthy and active lifestyle through cycling while offering exceptional customer service and expertise.

Visit us today to explore our wide selection of bikes and gear, or schedule a service appointment with our experienced mechanics.
